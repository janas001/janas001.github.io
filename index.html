WELCOME

Welcome to our cooking school "Easy Cooking",
located in Belgrade, Serbia. We are ranked as
the #1 cooking school in Europe for the past 5
years! Go to the tab contact to apply if you are
interested in gaining knowledge and confidence
in your cooking skills.

You will be able to learn many new recipes and
techniques, including both cooking, and also
decorating. We offer a variety of recipes for learning.
Cakes, pizzas, pastas, muffins, doughnuts, you
name it! It's up to you to decide what you
want to make. Our classes are held in small groups,
three times a week for four months. In the first two
weeks you will learn about correct terms and the use
of every kitchen utensil.

After that, the cooking and decorating portion
of the course starts. The teachers are some of
the best chefs in the world. Every project you
do is photographed so you could see the
progress you are making. We offer the
training in two cathegories: beginners & masters.
Join our classes to improve your cooking skills
and have fun while learning. Hope to see you
in our classes!
